Cardiology

At MediBuddy, our cardiology department is dedicated to providing top-notch heart care with precision and compassion. We offer advanced diagnostic services, preventive heart screenings, and expert consultation to help patients manage and improve their cardiovascular health. With experienced cardiologists and state-of-the-art technology, we ensure timely detection and effective treatment of heart-related conditions—because your heart deserves the best care.

Neurology

MediBuddy’s neurology department offers comprehensive care for disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system. Our skilled neurologists specialize in diagnosing and treating conditions like migraines, epilepsy, stroke, and neurodegenerative diseases. Using advanced diagnostic tools and a patient-first approach, we strive to deliver accurate assessments and effective treatments to improve quality of life and restore neurological health.

Hepatology

MediBuddy’s hepatology care focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of liver-related conditions. Our expert hepatologists treat diseases such as hepatitis, fatty liver, cirrhosis, and liver infections with precision and care. With modern diagnostic techniques and personalized treatment plans, we ensure each patient receives timely support to maintain optimal liver health and overall well-being.

Pediatrics

MediBuddy’s pediatrics department is dedicated to the comprehensive health and development of children. From newborns to adolescents, our pediatric specialists provide compassionate care, routine checkups, immunizations, and treatment for common childhood illnesses. We create a child-friendly environment to ensure young patients feel safe and supported throughout their healthcare journey.

Eye Care

MediBuddy’s Eye Care services offer advanced diagnostics and treatments for a wide range of vision and eye health concerns. Our experienced ophthalmologists provide routine eye exams, vision correction, cataract screening, and management of eye conditions with precision and care, ensuring your vision stays clear and protected.
